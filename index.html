ABOUT: MathMate

Identity

MathMate Tutoring, founded by Daniel Saltzman in Lincolnshire, Illinois, offers specialized math tutoring for children of all ages. Catering to the northwest suburbs of Chicago with in-person sessions and providing global access through Zoom, MathMate covers a wide range of topics from elementary math to multivariable calculus. With over 500 hours of tutoring experience, Saltzman and his team are dedicated to making math accessible and enjoyable, helping students improve their understanding and confidence in the subject.

Mission

At MathMate, our mission is to empower learners of all ages to achieve excellence in mathematics. We are committed to fostering a deep understanding and appreciation for math through personalized and engaging tutoring sessions. Our approach combines innovative teaching methods with a supportive learning environment, tailored to meet the unique needs and goals of each student. We believe that mastery of mathematical concepts not only enhances academic performance but also nurtures critical thinking and problem-solving skills vital for future success. Our dedicated team of expert tutors is passionate about inspiring confidence and curiosity in math, making it accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Offerings

Class Assistance

Whether your student is falling behind or aiming to excel, we can provide tailored support for them. We help struggling students bridge gaps and boost confidence. For high achieving students, we offer advanced coursework and enrichment opportunities, nurturing their passion for learning and academic success.

SAT/ACT Preparation

In today's competitive educational landscape, standardized tests like the SAT and ACT play crucial roles in college admissions. These tests assess a student's readiness for higher education and significantly impact future academic opportunities. This program will help navigate this important step.

AP Preparation

We understand the immense value of AP exams and are committed to guiding students towards exceptional performance. AP tests are crucial for high school students, offering the potential to earn college credits. Excelling in these exams saves time, money, and strengthens college applications.
